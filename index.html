About Us

Company Profile

Tsehaytu Consulting Architects and Engineers offers a comprehensive range of services, including architectural design, structural engineering, electrical engineering, sanitary engineering, landscape design, interior design, supervision, contract management, and preparation of Bills of Quantities and specifications. Our skilled team is dedicated to providing quality work that earns the trust of our clients.

Vision

Our vision is to become a leading consultancy firm recognized for our expertise, quality, integrity, and excellence in the construction industry.

Mission

We are committed to delivering sustainable and innovative technical solutions that positively impact society and the environment.

Objective

Our objective is to provide exceptional consultancy services in design, construction supervision, quality control, and contract administration for both local and international markets.
